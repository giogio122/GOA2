Mr Beast

James Stephen "Jimmy" Donaldson[a] (born May 7, 1998), commonly known by his online alias MrBeast, is an American YouTuber, media personality, and businessman. Donaldson produces high-paced YouTube videos built around elaborate challenges and grandiose philanthropic efforts, that are noted for their high production values.[7] With more than 446 million YouTube subscribers, his main channel is the most subscribed on YouTube.[8][9] He is also the third-most-followed creator on TikTok, with over 119.3 million followers. 450M Subcriber

T-Series

Super Cassettes Industries Private Limited,[a][1][3] doing business as T-Series,[b] is an Indian record label and film studio founded by Gulshan Kumar on 11 July 1983.[5] It is primarily known for Hindi film soundtracks[5] and Indi-pop music.[6] T-Series is the largest music record label in India, with up to a 35% share of the Indian music market.[7] As of October 2025, T-Series also owns and operates the most-viewed and the second most-subscribed YouTube channel, with over 305 million subscribers[8] and 314 billion views.[9] While best known as a music label, T-Series has also produced more than 100 films, and has established itself as a leading film production company in Hindi cinema. 307M Subcriber

Cocomelon

Cocomelon (/koʊkoʊmɛlən/, stylized as CoComelon) is a children's YouTube channel operated by Candle Media-owned Moonbug Entertainment. The channel specializes in 3D animation videos of traditional nursery rhymes and original children's songs. As of July 2025, Cocomelon is the 3rd most-subscribed and 2nd most-viewed channel on YouTube.[1] The channel was launched in 2006, and rebranded to Cocomelon in 2018. It has since expanded globally in multiple languages, with spinoff series, Netflix TV shows, merchandising, special events, and ongoing product diversification. It was purchased by Candle Media in 2021 for a reported $3 billion. 198M Subcriber
