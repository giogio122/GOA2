1.Grilled salmon with salad

Season salmon with salt, pepper, and olive oil. Grill on medium heat until golden and flaky. Prepare fresh salad with greens and vegetables. Serve salmon hot, topped with lemon juice, alongside the salad for balance and light herbs for freshness.

2.Margerita Pizza

Margherita pizza ingredients include fresh pizza dough, ripe tomato sauce made from tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, extra virgin olive oil, fresh basil leaves, salt, and sometimes a small amount of garlic. These simple ingredients create a classic, fresh, and balanced Italian pizza flavor.
